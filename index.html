GRADUATE THESIS PROJECT | TEMPLE ISLAND, BRISTOL

Beacon

Re-Housing the Bristol Flyers

A propsal for a new stadium for the Birstol Flyer’s, Bristol’s only professional basketball team. The design aims to challenge the current way of imagining stadia by breaking down the typology. Reducing the effects of codification and commercialisation in sport, the design aims to bring back the essence of what a ‘spectacle’ entails. By establishing the building in it’s context and adressing the very root sport plays in collective identification, the site is designed to enrich the with an accessible public sports complex.

BASIL SPENCE PROJECT | FILTON, BRISTOL

Flagship[

Sustainability through Versitility

The propsoal aims to manage how sustainablity can be approached in contemporary design. using long lasting materials and an effeciantly versatile layout, it becomes possible to ensure the increase in life of the building, reducing the embodied CO2 per year. Endless reconfiguration of the movable CLT boxes underneath the skin of the structure is a celebration of the aerospace heritage of Filton and also allows the build to constantly adapt to the changing needs of the community.

3RD YEAR PROJECT | CAMPO ST. APOSTOLI, VENICE

Venetian Fields

Mixed Use Buildings

The brief was to design a mixed use contemporary building that sits within a Venetian Campo (or square). Functions include a gondola workshop, gelateria, tavern, pizzeria, medical centre and housing.

3RD YEAR PROJECT | POPLAR, LONDON

Project Poplar

Bridge and Public Building

Poplar HARCA (Housing and regeneration community association) were looking to unlock the potential of the site, a unique opportunity to connect different communities that are currently ‘cut’ off by the Limehouse canal.

2ND YEAR PROJECT | GREEN PARK, BATH

Medical Centre

A New Medical Centre for Bath

A proposal for an unassuming, non monumental, contemporary building medical centre in Bath that is at it’s core: A community hub. Designed to reflect the classical architecture of the city and engage with river.

2ND YEAR PROJECT
