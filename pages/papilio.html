1ST YEAR PROJECT | UNI OF BATH, BATH

Papilio

An Exhibition Pavilion

The brief called for the design a pavilion sited on the lake overlooked by the university: to display the work of architecture students and act as a manifestation of the department. The design was motivated by wishing to provide a clear path for circulation as needed by an exhibition space. The outer skin of the pavilion was designed to be movable. The transparent nature of the design was a deliberate attempt to remind the viewer that they are atop a lake. The corten doors are designed to slide across the elevations of the pavilion as and when required.
